FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I book a bus ticket on your site?

Booking a bus ticket is easy and straightforward. Simply visit our website, select the 'Book Tickets' option, enter your departure and arrival locations, travel dates, and the number of passengers. Click on the 'Search Buses' button, and you'll be presented with a list of available buses for your route. Choose the one that suits your preferences and proceed to the payment page to confirm your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods to provide you with a convenient booking experience. You can pay for your bus ticket using credit cards, debit cards, and popular online payment platforms like PayPal.

Is it necessary to create an account to book a ticket?

Yes, it is necessary to create an account to book a ticket, in order to track all your tickets.

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your reservation, subject to the terms and conditions of your ticket type and the bus operator's policy. Simply log in to your account, go to the 'My Bookings' section, and choose the booking you wish to cancel or modify. Please note that cancellation or modification fees may apply depending on the time left before departure.

Will I receive a confirmation of my booking?

Yes, once you successfully complete the booking process, you will receive an email confirmation containing all the details of your reservation. We recommend keeping this email for your reference when boarding the bus.

Can I book a bus ticket for someone else?

Yes, you can book a bus ticket for someone else. During the booking process, you have the option to enter the passenger's details, including their name and contact information. However, it's essential to ensure that the passenger has a copy of the booking confirmation for a smooth boarding process.
